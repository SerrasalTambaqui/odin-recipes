Parmalet



Ingredients


  • Eggs
  • Water
  • Olive Oil
  • Parmigiano-Reggiano Cheese
  • Salt and Black Pepper
  • Cayenne Pepper

Steps


  1. Crack eggs into a mixing bowl, add water and whisk together until beaten
  2. Drizzle olive oil into a nonstick skillet and evenly grate cheese into the skillet approximately 1/2-inch deep
  3. Plan pan over medium-high heat. WHen cheese starts to bubble and turn golden brown, pour eggs evenly over cheese. Reduce to low heat and sprinkle with seasonings
  4. Cover and cook on low until they are set
  5. Remove pan from heat and fold the parmalet in half
