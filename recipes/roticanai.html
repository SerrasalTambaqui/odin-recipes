Roti Canai



Ingredients


For the Roti Canai


  • Flour
  • White sugar
  • Salt
  • Eggs
  • Butter
  • Water
  • Vegetable Oil

Steps


  1. Combine flour, sugar, salt, egg, melted butter, and water. Mix until a shaggy dough forms
  2. Knead until a smotth elastic dough forms
  3. Divide into 8 equal pieces and form each one into a ball and coat them with vegetable oil
  4. Flatten each ball and rub melted butter on top
  5. Add some butter to a skillet over medium or medium-low heat, transfer the roti canai and cook until browned, flaky, and crispy on the outside
